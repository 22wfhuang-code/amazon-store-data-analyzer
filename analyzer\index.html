Product Playbook

View metric definitions, problem logic, and actionable optimization steps.

Metric Definition

GMV reflects sales scale and is a core signal of growth and traffic monetization.

Problem Logic

If GMV grows while profit stalls, ad costs, discount intensity, or return costs may be rising together.

Action Advice

1. Split GMV by product line and scale ASINs with both stable conversion and strong gross margin.

2. Replace broad discounts with time-limited tiered offers to avoid margin erosion.
